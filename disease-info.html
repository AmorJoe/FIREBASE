Skin Conditions Library

Learn more about common skin conditions and how to manage them.

Acne

A common skin condition that happens when hair follicles under the skin become clogged. Sebum and dead skin cells plug the pores, and outbreaks of lesions (often called pimples or zits) can happen.

Eczema

Also known as atopic dermatitis, this condition makes your skin red and itchy. It's common in children but can occur at any age. It's long-lasting (chronic) and tends to flare periodically.

Psoriasis

A skin disease that causes red, itchy scaly patches, most commonly on the knees, elbows, trunk and scalp. It's a common, long-term (chronic) disease with no cure.
